# Privacy Policy for Wordsearch by Cloverbrook Games

**Effective Date:** July 5, 2026
**Last Updated:** July 5, 2026

Aaron Thomas ("we," "our," or "us") built Wordsearch by Cloverbrook Games ("the App") as a paid-unlock app with no subscriptions or ads. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

- The App does not require an account, sign-in, or profile of any kind.
- The App works fully offline. There is no backend server, and the App does not send your usage, gameplay, or device data anywhere.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies of any kind.
- All settings (text size, sound preferences, selected color theme) are stored only in local storage on your own device, and are never transmitted to us or anyone else.
- We do not access your camera, photos, contacts, or location.

---

## 2. In-App Purchases

The App offers a single one-time purchase ("Unlock All Categories & Themes," $2.99) that unlocks additional content within the App. This purchase is processed entirely through Apple's App Store using Apple's standard in-app purchase system.

We use **RevenueCat**, a third-party service, to verify and manage the state of your purchase (i.e., to check whether you have already unlocked the content). RevenueCat receives a device-generated anonymous identifier and your purchase/transaction status from Apple's App Store — it does not receive your name, email, address, or payment details. We never see or store your payment information (card number, billing address, etc.) directly; that is handled entirely by Apple.

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| Apple App Store / StoreKit | Processes the in-app purchase and payment | [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |
| RevenueCat | Verifies purchase/entitlement status | [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/) |

## 3. How We Use Information

Because we do not collect personal information, there is no user data for us to use, sell, rent, or share for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since the App collects no personal information from any user, there is no data to review, delete, or otherwise manage on behalf of a child.
