## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need an account to play?**
No. Back Then Trivia works without any account, sign-in, or ongoing internet connection. The App occasionally checks for new trivia questions in the background, but always falls back to the questions already on your device if you're offline.

**How do I unlock more decades?**
From the home screen or Settings, tap "Unlock All Decades" — a single one-time purchase ($2.99) unlocks everything permanently. No subscriptions.

**I bought the unlock on another device (or reinstalled the app) — how do I get it back?**
Open Settings and tap "Restore Purchase." As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID (or Google account) you originally purchased with, your unlock will be restored automatically.

**How does a round work?**
Pick a decade, then answer eight multiple-choice questions about it, one at a time. Tap an answer to see right away whether it's correct — the right answer is always shown, whether you got it or not. If you're stuck, tap "Skip this question" to move on without answering. There's no score or timer — play at your own pace.

**How do I change the text size?**
Open Settings from the home screen and choose Small, Medium, Large, or Huge under Text Size.

**How do I turn off the background music or sound effects?**
Tap the music note or speaker icon in the top right of the home screen (or the question screen), or turn them off in Settings.
