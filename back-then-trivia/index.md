# Privacy Policy for Back Then Trivia by Cloverbrook Games

**Effective Date:** July 8, 2026
**Last Updated:** July 8, 2026

Aaron Thomas ("we," "our," or "us") built Back Then Trivia by Cloverbrook Games ("the App") as a paid-unlock app with no subscriptions or ads. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

- The App does not require an account, sign-in, or profile of any kind.
- There is no backend server, and the App does not send your usage, gameplay, or device data anywhere.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies of any kind.
- All settings (text size, sound preferences, selected color theme) are stored only in local storage on your own device, and are never transmitted to us or anyone else.
- We do not access your camera, photos, contacts, or location.

### Trivia content updates

The App periodically checks a public content file (hosted on GitHub Pages) for updated trivia questions, so new questions can be added without requiring an app update. This check happens automatically in the background and does not send any information about you or your device beyond what any standard web request includes (such as your IP address, which is logged by GitHub's hosting infrastructure the same way any website's server logs would be — we do not access, collect, or review these logs ourselves). If this check fails or you are offline, the App simply continues using the trivia questions already on your device — gameplay is never interrupted or blocked by this.

## 2. In-App Purchases

The App offers a single one-time purchase ("Unlock All Decades," $2.99) that unlocks additional content within the App. This purchase is processed entirely through Apple's App Store or Google Play (depending on which device you use), using that store's standard in-app purchase system.

We use **RevenueCat**, a third-party service, to verify and manage the state of your purchase (i.e., to check whether you have already unlocked the content). RevenueCat receives a device-generated anonymous identifier and your purchase/transaction status from Apple's App Store or Google Play — it does not receive your name, email, address, or payment details. We never see or store your payment information (card number, billing address, etc.) directly; that is handled entirely by Apple or Google.

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| Apple App Store / StoreKit | Processes the in-app purchase and payment (iOS) | [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |
| Google Play Billing | Processes the in-app purchase and payment (Android) | [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| RevenueCat | Verifies purchase/entitlement status | [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/) |
| GitHub Pages | Hosts the public trivia content file the App periodically checks for updates | [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement) |
