## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need an account to play?**
No. Wordsearch works fully offline with no account, sign-in, or internet connection required.

**How do I unlock more categories and color themes?**
From the home screen or Settings, tap "Unlock All Categories & Themes" — a single one-time purchase ($2.99) unlocks everything permanently. No subscriptions.

**I bought the unlock on another device (or reinstalled the app) — how do I get it back?**
Open Settings and tap "Restore Purchase." As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID you originally purchased with, your unlock will be restored automatically.

**How do I change the text size?**
Open Settings from the home screen and choose Small, Medium, Large, or Huge under Text Size.

**How do I turn off the background music or sound effects?**
Tap the music note or speaker icon in the top right of the home screen (or the puzzle screen), or turn them off in Settings.
